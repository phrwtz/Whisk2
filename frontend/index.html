How To Play

Remote (two computers)

  • Player 1 joins first and chooses Remote mode.
  • Player 2 joins from the same URL.
  • Both players choose a square each turn.
  • Moves reveal together when both have moved.
  • Build 3, 4, or 5 in a row to score points.

Local (one computer)

  • Player 1 joins and chooses Local mode.
  • One device alternates turns: O, then X, then O, etc.
  • Each click commits immediately in local mode.
  • Build 3, 4, or 5 in a row to score points.
  • First side to 50+ points wins.
